About Us

We are students at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (TJHSST), and created this website to help people to be able to find celebrities, politicians, and criminals (three of the most searched categories) all in one place. Our webstie is powered by BigParser and all of our data on people is publicly available and can be found here for public use. We are hoping that our website provides you with the informaiton you were looking for in a convenient and effective manner. If you could support us by following any of the social media profiles located at the bottom of this page, it would be a great help, and we would be able to continue to make websites to help you in the future.
